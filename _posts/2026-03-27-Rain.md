Rain, has forever been used as an allegory, metaphor, symbol, or even just as a foil for many works of literature and culture. But today, apart from catching a cold from standing in it for too long, I wanted to capture it as a function of light.

I think that its best more often than not, to keep things simple. Here, I found that by keeping it "simple", the foreground will open up and oftentimes tell enough of a story. 

Shot on my Nikon D600!
